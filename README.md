Jaki-Netwood: The Nano-Bio Operating System ?? Systems for Cancer Detection & Immune Priming
?? Project Overview
Jaki-Netwood is a cutting-edge bio-hybrid robotics platform designed to program, control, and monitor Magnetotactic Bacteria for advanced medical intervention. By leveraging NVIDIA's high-performance computing and AI-driven simulations , the system achieves autonomous navigation at a 500nm scale to detect, scan, and neutralize oncological threats while training the body's natural immune memory.

?? Core Technical Pillars
1. Nano-Scale Physical Simulation (Powered by NVIDIA)
Scale Precision: Specialized simulation of bio-agents at the 500nm scale, navigating complex fluid dynamics within the human circulatory system.

NVIDIA Isaac Sim: Utilizing Isaac Sim's physics engine to model micro-scale forces (Brownian motion, fluid viscosity) for high-fidelity bacteria behavior.

Digital Twin via Omniverse: Creation of a real-time Digital Twin of the patient's internal environment to predict and optimize swarm trajectories.

2. Autonomous Navigation & Control
Magnetic Actuation: Precise steering of Magnetosomes using programmed electromagnetic fields.

AI Trajectory Optimization: Deep learning models trained to correct paths in real-time based on live feedback from Nano-Imaging sensors.

3. Early Warning & Immune Memory Priming
Nano-Scanning: Bacteria function as biological scanners, capturing the molecular "fingerprint" of cancer cells (approx. 10-30?m).

Immune Training: The system utilizes gathered data to "prime" the immune system, teaching T-cells to recognize and remember specific tumor signatures before the disease escalates.
